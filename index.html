Mayor's Hotline Explorer (last 30 days)

This interactive tool allows you to explore the last 30 days' submissions to the City of Boston's Mayor's 24-Hour Constituent Services Hotline. The displays are all inter-connected—click on a row in one of the bar charts to filter on that value. Click-and-drag to select a time range on the time graph to analyze a particular time range. Please note that it takes a moment to load the initial data.
